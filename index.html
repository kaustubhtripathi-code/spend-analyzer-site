FAQ

Fair questions.

How does it get my transactions without SMS or Account Aggregator access?

Your bank already emails you every transaction alert. Spend Analyzer connects to Gmail with a read-only scope, finds those alerts (and your card-statement PDFs), and parses them locally. Revoke access anytime from your Google account.

Does the AI see my bank data?

Only if you enable it, and even then it only receives merchant names and monthly aggregates — never raw transactions, account numbers, or balances. Turn AI off entirely with one setting and the rules + ML engine still categorizes everything at zero API cost.

Which banks work today?

HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Zerodha and UPI alerts, plus HDFC credit-card PDF statements. Each new bank is a small parser — SBI, Axis and Kotak are next. Request yours and it moves up the list.

What do I need to run it?

A Windows laptop (Mac/Linux launchers coming), Python and Node one-time setup, and a free Google Cloud OAuth credential — our 3-minute guide walks you through it. Then it's one double-click to start.

What happens to my data if I stop using it?

Nothing — that's the point. It's a file on your disk. Export everything to CSV anytime, or just delete the folder.
